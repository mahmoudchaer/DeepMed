Augmentation Levels Explained:
  • Level 1: Basic flips and minimal brightness/contrast changes
  • Level 2: Flips, slight rotations, and moderate brightness/contrast
  • Level 3: More rotation, scaling, shifting, and color adjustments
  • Level 4: Elastic transforms, noise, and significant geometric changes
  • Level 5: Maximum variation with all possible transforms
Higher levels create more diverse images but may introduce artifacts or unrealistic variations.
