Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

What is DeepMed and how does it work?

DeepMed is an AI-powered medical imaging platform that uses deep learning algorithms to analyze medical images such as X-rays, CT scans, MRIs, and ultrasounds. Our technology helps healthcare providers detect anomalies, assist in diagnoses, and improve patient outcomes.

When a medical image is uploaded to DeepMed, our AI models analyze the image and identify patterns that may indicate various medical conditions. The system then generates detailed reports with highlighted areas of interest, suggested findings, and relevant medical references.

How accurate is DeepMed's AI?

DeepMed's AI models have been validated through rigorous clinical studies and have shown high accuracy rates for the conditions they're trained to detect. For most applications, our models achieve sensitivity and specificity rates of over 90%, which is comparable to or exceeds the performance of experienced radiologists in controlled studies.

However, it's important to note that DeepMed is designed to be a diagnostic aid, not a replacement for healthcare professionals. Our technology works best when used as a second opinion or screening tool alongside professional medical judgment.

Is my medical data secure with DeepMed?

Yes, data security and patient privacy are our top priorities. DeepMed is fully HIPAA compliant and employs industry-leading security measures to protect all medical data, including:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest
  • SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure
  • Regular third-party security audits and penetration testing
  • Role-based access controls and detailed audit logs
  • Data center redundancy and disaster recovery protocols

We never share patient data with third parties without explicit consent, and our business model does not rely on data monetization.

How can I integrate DeepMed with my existing systems?

DeepMed offers several integration options to fit into your existing healthcare IT ecosystem:

  • DICOM Integration: Direct integration with PACS and modalities via DICOM protocol
  • HL7/FHIR Integration: Interoperability with EHR/EMR systems
  • REST API: Comprehensive API for custom integrations
  • SDK: Developer tools for seamless embedding in your applications
  • Standalone Deployment: On-premises solutions for organizations with specific infrastructure requirements

Our integration team works closely with your IT department to ensure smooth implementation with minimal disruption to your workflow.

What modalities and conditions does DeepMed support?

DeepMed currently supports the following imaging modalities:

  • X-ray (CR, DX)
  • CT scans
  • MRI
  • Ultrasound
  • Mammography

Our AI models can assist in detecting and analyzing various conditions, including but not limited to:

  • Pulmonary conditions (pneumonia, tuberculosis, COVID-19)
  • Fractures and bone abnormalities
  • Neurological conditions (stroke, hemorrhage, tumors)
  • Cardiovascular conditions
  • Oncological findings across multiple body regions

We are continuously expanding our capabilities with new models and supported conditions.

How much does DeepMed cost?

DeepMed offers flexible pricing options designed to accommodate healthcare providers of all sizes:

  • Pay-per-use: Ideal for smaller practices with low volume needs
  • Monthly subscription: For medium-sized practices with predictable volumes
  • Enterprise: Customized pricing for large healthcare systems with high volume requirements

All plans include access to our core AI models, with optional add-ons for specialized models and custom integrations. For detailed pricing information tailored to your organization's needs, please contact our sales team.

How do I get started with DeepMed?

Getting started with DeepMed is simple:

  1. Request a demo through our website
  2. Work with our team to determine the best implementation approach for your needs
  3. Set up your account and configure integrations with your existing systems
  4. Complete our brief training program to ensure your team can maximize the benefits of our platform
  5. Start using DeepMed in your clinical workflow

Most organizations can be up and running with DeepMed in as little as 2-4 weeks, depending on integration requirements and organizational readiness.
